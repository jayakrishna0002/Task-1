A website is a collection of web pages and related content that is identified by a common domain name and published on at least one web server. Websites are typically dedicated to a particular topic or purpose, such as news, education, commerce, entertainment, or social media. MERN stands for MongoDB, Express.js, React.js, and Node.js. It is a popular stack for building full-stack web applications. The MERN stack is known for its ability to allow developers to use a single language, JavaScript, throughout the entire development process.
